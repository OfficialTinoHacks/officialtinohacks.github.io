What is TinoHacks?

TinoHacks II is a two day long, 350 person hackathon hosted by Cupertino High School students where both new and experienced programmers can work together on projects, learn new skills, and have a splendid time. TinoHacks I happened on April 15-16, 2017, and TinoHacks is coming back on April 7-8, 2018 with our second event! It is backed by Hack +, a 501 (c)(3)public charity registered in CA. All donations are tax - deductible. Please feel free to email us at hello@tinohacks.tech if you have any questions or concerns.
