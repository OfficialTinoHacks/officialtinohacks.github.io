Frequently Asked Questions

What is TinoHacks?

TinoHacks is a two day hackathon where both new and experienced programmers can work together on projects, learn new skills, and have splendid time.

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is a 24 to 38 hour long coding marathon event where people can make the most bizarre ideas into reality. Over the course of the weekend, students collaborate to create projects, listen to exquisite workshop presentations, exercise their programming abilities, and of course, have tons of fun!

Where and when is it?

TinoHacks will take place at 9:00 am on Saturday March 4th until 1:00 pm on Sunday March 5th. Stay tuned for our venue reveal!

How much does it cost?

Thanks to our sponsors, this event is completely free! This includes all the food, swag, and fantabulous prizes.

What will I need?

  • Laptop

  • Some form of identification

  • Signed and completed waiver

  • Chargers

  • Blanket/Sleeping Bag

  • Toiletries

  • Anything you need for your idea. Some hardware will be available to rent for the event.

Will there be adult supervision?

Yes.

Do I need any programming experience?

No! In fact, TinoHacks is a great way to begin your programming experience! A basic understanding is recommended, but we will have workshops and mentors to help you make your project idea a reality.
